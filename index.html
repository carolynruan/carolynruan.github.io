About

Hi, I'm Carolyn! I'm an undergrad at Caltech studying computer science. I'm broadly interested in machine learning, software engineering, and computational modeling. I've worked on (a) applying temporal sequence modeling to chemistry to predict C-H oxidation regioselectivity, and (b) modeling chemical reaction networks with ODEs in the Reisman Lab at Caltech.

In my free time, I play D3 basketball at Caltech and am an avid reader and photographer.
